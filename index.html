How to Play

  • The aim of this game is to guess the country in as few guesses as possible.
  • Every day a new country is chosen and you get 10 hints, one at a time to guess it.
  • e.g if the country of the day is "United Kingdom", the first hint might be "It is located in Europe".
  • Enter your guess after each hint. You have one guess per hint.
  • If you guess correctly, the flag is revealed and you win!
  • If you use all 10 hints, you lose and the answer and flag are revealed.
  • The game resets with a new country every day at 12:00 AM British time.
  • Disclaimer: This game only includes countries that are full members of the United Nations. Disputed territories or partially recognized states are not part of the game.
